Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-05-13

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord IDs: Guild ID, User ID, Channel ID
  • Intro Questions & Responses: The text you configure and users submit

2. How We Use Your Data

To post introductions, manage “Newcomer” & “Member” roles, and persist your configured questions.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on the Bot’s host. Access is restricted to the Bot maintainer only.

4. Data Retention

Data remains until you remove the Bot from your server or request deletion via our support server.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data by joining our support server. We will honor such requests within 30 days.

Contact & Support

For privacy inquiries or data requests, join our support server: WelcomeGate Support
